MyArhiled

Android app for Arhiled light controllers

About

Welcome to MyArhiled, the ultimate way to control your smart light controllers. Our application allows you to setup and control your light controllers with just a few taps.

In manual mode, you can easily adjust each channel level individually to create the perfect atmosphere for any occasion. In schedule mode, you can easily adjust each channel to a specific level for a specific time. This allows you to create multiple pre-defined schedules and easily switch between them from the device control screen.

Our application also allows you to group multiple devices together and control them as one. This makes it easy to control multiple light controllers with just one tap.

With our intuitive user interface and simple setup process, MyArhiled is the perfect way to take your light controllers to the next level. Download it now and start creating the perfect atmosphere in your home or office.
