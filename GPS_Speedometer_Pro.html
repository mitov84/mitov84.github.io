About

GPS Speedometer Pro is a mobile application that helps you effortlessly track your daily travels. All you have to do is start the tracking session and you can then close the app; it will continue to record your route in the background. Whether you need to pause, resume, or stop recording, you can do so with just a few taps. Your saved routes will be visible in the "History" section of the app, so you can easily review your progress anytime.
Download GPS Speedometer Pro today and start keeping track of your journeys!
