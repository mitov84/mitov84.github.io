Fluid Control

Control FluidNC CNC machines with Android phone

About

Android mobile application to control your FluidNC CNC machine. Features jog control, terminal, spindle/mist/flood control, Z probe, custom macro definitions. Can send .nc files to machine, display progress, program flow control, alerts on job completion.
Ultimate remote control for your FluidNC CNC machine.
