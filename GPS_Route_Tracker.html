GPS Route Tracker

Android mobile application for tracking your daily routes.

About

GPS Route Tracker is free mobile application that enables you to record your daily tracks. After you start the tracking you can close the app and it will continue to record your route in background. You can pause, resume and stop recording at any time. Your saved routes will be visible in the "History" section of the application.
